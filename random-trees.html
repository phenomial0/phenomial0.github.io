RANDOM TREES

Phenomial Digital Art

This project was inspired by the recursive organic beauty of natural trees and roots, a structure often emulated in the conceptual world of algorithms. The program used to generate these images was based on my 2011 work on randomized robotic motion planning, with the branching paths representing for example possible trajectories of a car through a cluttered area.
